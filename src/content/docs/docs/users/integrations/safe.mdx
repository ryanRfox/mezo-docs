[Safe](https://safe.global/) (formerly Gnosis Safe) is the most widely used multisig wallet in crypto. Mezo has a native Safe deployment at [safe.mezo.org](https://safe.mezo.org), managed by [Den](https://www.onchainden.com/).

A multisig (multi-signature) wallet requires multiple people to approve a transaction before it executes. Instead of one person controlling funds with a single private key, a group defines rules like "3 out of 5 signers must approve."

<LinkButton href="https://safe.mezo.org" target="_blank">Open Safe on Mezo</LinkButton>

## Getting started

1. Go to [safe.mezo.org](https://safe.mezo.org).
2. Connect your wallet.
3. Click **Create new Safe Account**.
4. Add the wallet addresses of your co-signers.
5. Set the confirmation threshold — the number of approvals required to execute a transaction (e.g., 2 out of 3).
6. Review and deploy your Safe.

Once deployed, you can send and receive BTC and other tokens on Mezo through your Safe, with every transaction requiring the approval threshold you configured.

## Features

- **Multi-signature security** — Require multiple signers to approve transactions before execution.
- **Modules and guards** — Customize your Safe with pluggable modules for spending limits, recovery, automated transactions, and more.
- **Ecosystem access** — Interact with dApps on Mezo directly from your Safe using the built-in app browser.
- **Watch accounts** — Monitor any Safe account without connecting a wallet.
